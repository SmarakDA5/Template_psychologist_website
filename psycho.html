Services

  • Psychological Assessment and Diagnosis: Evaluation of cognitive, emotional, and behavioral functioning. This includes specialized testing for Intellectual Quotient (IQ), Neuropsychological deficits, and Personality profiles.
  • Psychotherapy and Treatment Modalities: Providing individual, group, or relational therapy sessions. Key evidence-based approaches include Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Psychodynamic approaches, and Interpersonal Therapy (IPT).
  • Relational and Systemic Functioning: Focused interventions, such as Couples Therapy and Family Therapy, designed to address dynamics, communication, and conflict within groups or relationship systems.
  • Applied Consultation and Specialized Fields: The application of psychological principles in non-clinical settings, including Forensic Psychology, Industrial-Organizational (I-O) Psychology, School Psychology, and Health Psychology.
